Our Roofing Services

Everything your roof needs — from emergency storm repairs to full tear-off and replacement across the DFW Metroplex.

Roof Replacement

When repairs aren't enough, we do full tear-off and replacement with premium materials built for the Texas climate. From architectural shingles rated for high winds to standing seam metal roofing that handles the brutal DFW heat, we install systems designed to last. Most residential roofs completed in 1–3 days with minimal disruption to your daily life.

  • Full tear-off and disposal of old roofing materials
  • Architectural (dimensional) shingle installation
  • Standing seam and corrugated metal roofing
  • Synthetic underlayment and ice/water shield
  • New drip edge, ridge vent, and flashing
  • Manufacturer warranty + workmanship guarantee

Storm & Hail Damage Repair

North Texas is one of the most hail-prone regions in the country. When severe weather rolls through Fort Worth and the surrounding areas, your roof takes the hit. We respond fast to assess the damage, document everything for your insurance company, and get your roof restored to pre-storm condition. From softball-sized hail dents to wind-stripped shingles, we've seen and fixed it all.

  • Comprehensive hail and wind damage assessment
  • Full photo documentation for insurance claims
  • Shingle replacement and partial re-roofing
  • Ridge cap and valley repair
  • Damaged vent and flashing replacement
  • Complete storm damage restoration

Emergency Tarping & Leak Repair

When a storm blows through at 2 AM and your ceiling starts dripping, you need help now — not next week. We provide same-day emergency tarping and temporary repairs to stop water intrusion and prevent further interior damage. Once the immediate threat is handled, we schedule a full inspection and permanent repair.

  • 24/7 emergency response during storm season
  • Same-day tarping for exposed roof decks
  • Active leak diagnosis and temporary repair
  • Board-up service for severe damage
  • Interior water damage assessment
  • Follow-up permanent repair scheduling
