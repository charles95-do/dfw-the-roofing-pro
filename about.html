About DFW The Roofing Pro

Fort Worth's trusted storm damage roofing experts for over 15 years.

Storm Damage Experts, Fort Worth Roots

DFW The Roofing Pro was founded right here in Fort Worth with a mission that hasn't changed in over 15 years: give DFW homeowners a roofing contractor they can actually trust. Someone who shows up when they say they will, gives an honest estimate, does the work right, and stands behind it long after the last nail is driven.

We're not a franchise or a storm-chasing outfit that blows into town after a hailstorm and disappears. We're a locally owned Fort Worth company. Our families live here. Our kids go to school here. When you call us, you get someone who knows your neighborhood — not a call center three states away.

North Texas weather is no joke. Between the spring hailstorms, summer heat, and the occasional tornado, your roof takes a beating year after year. That's why storm damage restoration has always been at the core of what we do. We've helped thousands of DFW homeowners navigate the insurance claims process and get their roofs restored to pre-storm condition — often at no out-of-pocket cost beyond their deductible.

From emergency tarping at 2 AM to full roof replacements, we treat every job — big or small — with the same level of care and professionalism. That's how we've built our reputation across Fort Worth, Arlington, Mansfield, and communities throughout the Metroplex.
